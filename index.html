Dr. Ravi Zacharias

Skeptic turned apologist

Here's a time line of Dr. Zacharias's life:

  • 1946: Born in Madras, India
  • 1963: Attempts to commit suicide
  • 1966: Immigrates to Canada
  • 1972: Earns undergraduate degree from the Ontario Bible School (now Tyndale University)
  • 1972: Marries Margaret "Margie" Reynolds
  • 1976: Earns his Masters in Divinity from Trinity International University
  • 1990: Participates in guided study at Ridley Hall, a Church of England theological school in Cambridge
  • 2017: Accused of exaggerating academic credentials such as use of the title "Dr." despite lacking PhD qualification
  • 2020: Diagnosed with malignant, rare cancer in his spine
  • 2020: Dies at the age of 74
  • 2020: Accused of multiple accounts of sexual misconduct

A coherent worldview must be able to satisfactorily answer four questions: that of origin, meaning of life, morality, and destiny. While every major religion makes exclusive claims about truth, the Christian faith is unique in its ability to answer all four of these questions.

-- Ravi Zacharias
